Best Ann Arbor Meals

Breakfast

Angelos:

Angelos is my favorite breakfast resturant. The best thing to order there are their omelettes. The omelette contains 3 eggs and a choice of 4 add-ins free of charge. Heres one of my creations.

Brueggers Bagels:

Additionally, I love Brueggers Bagels. Brueggers Bagels has both great coffee and bagels. It is a gerat breakfast meal to have!

Wilmas:

Lastly, Wilmas is always a great option. They are a healthy place focused on good food. The Egg Sandwhich is my favorite breakfast item there, but they also have amaizng brunch items too!
